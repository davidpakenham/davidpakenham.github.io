About

Panama Sports Club is for the Outdoor + Dancefloor lovers. A laid-back, Independent lifestyle brand built around the ethos of 'active by day, club by night'. The logo is both clean and confident. Using a simple slicing technique the logo leans to the right indicating speed and movement.

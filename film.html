About

That Old Feeling is a graduate film directed by Séan Maher about a young rural man's love for jazz. I was tasked with creating film titles and printed ephemera. The typographic layout borrows from the vernacular of jazz record design in the 1960's. Adding to this, hand rendered letters including all the mistakes and grit allows this visual language to captures the raw, impulsive magic that is jazz.

Date: May, 2018
Disciplines: Motion, Print

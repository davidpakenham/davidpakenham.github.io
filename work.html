Vstage - Project Info

From organising to broadcasting, Vstage is a full service digital event solution. It enables you to host live events, connecting an unlimited amount of attendees in every country in the world under one secure platform. Vstage gives you the freedom to visually customise your event to fit your organisation’s needs. To reflect this Vstage is a living brand that can visually morph to fit it’s surroundings. The simple shapes that are used in the logo are repeated flexibly throughout the icon suite.

Date: June, 2021 Created at The Tenth Man

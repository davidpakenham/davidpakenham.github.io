About

Moving Type Festival is an international gathering of typographic exemplars – a collision of minds. It consists of talks, workshops and a student exhibition. This gathering of creativity is the concept that drives the identity. Abstract forms come together to create the letter M which results in a dynamic mark that can be used as a simple logo, to house or distort portraits of the speakers.

Date: September, 2017
Disciplines: Print, web, motion, art direction
Credits: Tom Gillan, motion production
Poster: 145/gsm silver paper
